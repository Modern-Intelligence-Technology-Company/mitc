# LESSONS.md — durable lessons

1. **Executable acceptance criteria** (even a small shell verifier) keep procurement writing packages from drifting into unfinished stubs without anyone noticing.
2. **Anchor public-sector narratives on primary sources** (e.g., official city releases); procurement evaluators recognize genericvendor language immediately.
3. **Separate “binding commercial terms” from technical volumes** until solicitation attachments exist—otherwise rework multiplies.
